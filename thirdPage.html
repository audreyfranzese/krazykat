About Us

Both owners have been grooming for over 25 years. Our mission is to provide your pet with a happy, safe, clean, and comfortable environment that promotes their overall health and wellbeing. Our staff prides themselves on striving towards the highest integrity, knowledge, skill, and excellence in the grooming profession. We are always eager to answer any questions, or address any concerns you may have about your pet. We understand how important your pet is to you. Our pets are part of our family and yours will be treated like our own.
